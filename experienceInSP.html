Current Experiences

Adjusting at first seemed to be a mission. Everything was new for an international student like myself to adjust to living in the new city. However, the moment I participated in SP online camp orientation, I was certain that this was going to be my second home for the next three years. During the orientation, we were able to partake in a variety of games as an ice-breaker between the students and teachers. We were also introduced to the wide range of workshops, and projects that were in place at SP. The karate workshop, in particular, stood out to me. Taking an interest in the club, I participated in the training provided by my senior senseis and gained plenty of new experiences and pleasant memories. I also joined the Archery Club for my CCA. I have to take an interview to get into the competitive team and I was able to get in. I am really satisfied with the result of me getting into Archery Club.

Courses Offereed in SOC

Diploma in Information Technology

Choosing to study the Diploma in Information Technology was a perfect fit for me as a person fascinated by all the modern technologies that the world has come to. By joining this program, I have gathered not only a great deal of bookish knowledge but also handfuls of real-life experience on different sectors concerning Information Technology. This course provides me with knowledge on a variety of sectors such as Enterprise Systems Development, Software Applications, and Data Engineering. Different electives from this Diploma include Software Development, Immersive Simulation Technology, and User Experience (UX) Design.

Other Courses Offered in SOC

DCIT

The Common ICT Programme focuses on teaching students the fundamentals of different sectors of ICT such as computing, and programming. This course also offers classes on Critical and Analytical Thinking, Communicating for Project Effectiveness, and Education and Career Guidance.

DAAA

This programme is perfect for students who want to immerse themselves in the world of robotics and artificial intelligence studies. This course offers different modules such as studies on Web Development, AI & Machine Learning, Data Engineering, Data Visualisation, and so on.

DISM

This Diploma is the best fit for students with an interest in keeping the internet a safe place for all users. In this course, students will have different modules, namely, Digital Forensics and Investigation, Secure Coding, Ethical Hacking and Defences, Security Policy and Incident Management, and so on.
